Forwards

Forwards are offensive players who are almost exclusively involved in the team's attack. Their primary role is to score goals and help create goal-scoring opportunities. While the team is in possession, forwards operate in the opposition box or just around it, being as close to the goal as possible. Forwards have a defensive role as well, as they are considered the first line of defense. Their defensive duties involve pressuring the ball and cutting off passing lanes to prevent an opposing team's advance. There are different positions that can be categorized as forwards: CF, SS, RW, and LW.
