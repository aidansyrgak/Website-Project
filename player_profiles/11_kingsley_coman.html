Background

Kingsley Coman played at Paris Saint-Germain's (PSG) youth academy. PSG is considered to be one of the top clubs in France. With some appearances for PSG, Coman acquired a Ligue 1 title. After that, Coman moved to Juventus, which is another top Italian club. With Juventus, Coman has won a Serie A title, even though his involvement was limited. Coman then got loaned out to Bayern Munich in 2015, and later bought by Bayern in 2017. Since then, Coman has won multiple German and European titles with Bayern.

Play Style

Kingsley Coman plays exclusively as a winger in RW or LW positions. As a sterotypical winger, he possesses incredible speed. He usually beats defenders using his acceleration alone, without having to resort to dribbling. However, his driblling ability is exceptional as well. Together with his speed, he uses feints to send a defender one way, and then zooms the other way, inveitably leaving the defender behind. Coman's 1v1 ability is extremely helpful for the team, as he can effortlessly get into the opposition box and create dangerous chances. His defensive contribution is noticeable as well. He is adept at pressuring the ball and is willing to run back to help the defenders.
