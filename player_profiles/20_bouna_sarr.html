Background

Sarr started his career at FC Gerland in the French city of Lyon. He started his professional career for Olympique Lyonnais in 2012, which also operates in Lyon. Sarr then moved to Marseille in 2015, where he became a consistent fullback. In 2020, Sarr joined Bayern Munich, where he played since.

Play Style

Bouna Sarr is a versatile soccer player who can play as both RB or RM. He is known for his speed, endurance, and contribution in attack. Sarr is a solid dribbler, and utilizes his footwork to outmaneuver the opposition and set up scoring opportunities. He is good at crossing the ball as well. His defensive contribution are dependable, as he frequently drops back to assist the team in defensive phases. Sarr is a diligent player who always strives to give his best. This persistence is an advantage to Bayern. Despite that, Sarr hasn't found major success with Bayern as he has been outshine by the team's other fullbacks. He has been mostly used as Bayern's rotation player.
