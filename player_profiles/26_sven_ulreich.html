Background

Ulreich began his career at SG Schorndorf. He joined Stuttgart in 1998, where he played for the team's youth academy. He kept playing for Stuttgart, even as he made his professional debut. In 2015, he signed for Bayern Munich. In 2018, Ulreich became a starting goalkeeper and won a Bundesliga title with the team. Since then, Ulreich kept playing for Bayern, mostly as a second-choice Goalkeeper behind either Manuel Neuer or Yann Sommer.

Play Style

Sven Ulreich has been Bayern's solid backup goalkeeper for a very long time. He has mastered goalkeeping fundamentals, being able to properly block, catch, and kick the ball. He is considered a defensive goalkeeper and doesn't go out of his box as much as Manuel Neuer. Ulreich is often criticized for his mediocre passing, which often led to dangerous situations for the team. However, as a backup goalkeeper, he performs all his duties on the top level and is able to deliver when the team needs him the most.
