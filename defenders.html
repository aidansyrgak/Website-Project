Defenders

Defenders are players whose primary responsibility is to prevent an opposing team from scoring goals. Their role is to organize the team's back line, intercept passes and shots, and tackle the ball from opposition forwards. They do anything to keep the ball as far away from their goal as possible. In possession play, however, they contribute to the team's offense as well. They play out from the back and make long passes to forwards. Additionally, defenders who operate on the flanks are required to run up the field and assists the attack during possession play. Positions that can be classified as defenders are: CB, RB, and LB.
