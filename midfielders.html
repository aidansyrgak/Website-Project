Midfielders

Midfielders are considered to be the heart of the team. They are a great contribution both offensively and defensively. They are required to run up and down the field, depending on whether the team is in possession or defending. They are orchestrators during possession play, dictating the pace of the game, recycling the ball, and using their passing skills and spatial awareness to create offensive plays. In defense, their role is to intercept and tackle the ball from the opposition, making sure that their defenders do as little work as possible. Midfielder positions include: AM, CM, LM, RM, and DM.
