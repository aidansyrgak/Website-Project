Goalkeepers

Goalkeepers stay in the team's goal and are supposed to block or catch any shots that make it past the defense. Goalkeepers are only allowed to use their hands inside their box, so they will spend most of the game time in there. In Bayern, however, it is not unusual for Goalkeepers to run out of their box to interecept any long passes from opposing teams. Additionally, they are a great passing choice to recycle the ball to during intense opposition pressure.
